Empowering Financial Literacy: Our Journey at the Athena Hackathon 2023

A mobile app designed to help young people to become financially literate. This project won the Athena Virtual Hackathon 2023.

By Molly, Maryan, Emma, Linda and Rosanna

Imagine trying to navigate rent, student loans, and everyday expenses without a clue where your money is going. This isn't a hypothetical scenario for many young adults ‚Äì it's their everyday reality. A staggering 59% of young people are considered financially illiterate, a statistic that underscores the widespread struggle to manage money effectively. This lack of financial knowledge can lead to accumulating debt, and difficulty saving for the future. At the Athena Hackathon 2023, our team decided to tackle this challenge head-on. Driven by a shared mission to empower young adults with financial understanding, we created CashSmart ‚Äì a mobile app designed to put financial literacy in the hands of the next generation.

The Development Process

üñå Based on our research insights, we conducted an affinity mapping exercise to identify key themes and brainstorm solutions to address the identified problems. For example, our user interviews revealed that many young adults struggled with understanding compound interest. This led us to create a dedicated module within CashSmart explaining the concept in simple terms, using real-world examples and interactive simulations.

üí≠Next, we brainstormed the brand image, deciding on the colors, shapes, and logos that would enhance the user experience. Additionally, we mapped out the user flow through the app to ensure that each feature was integrated in an intuitive and accessible manner.

üé® We knew CashSmart needed to feel trustworthy and approachable, so we developed a comprehensive design system. We chose a color palette of blues and greens to evoke a sense of calm and financial security. Our typography emphasizes clarity and readability. One of our key design decisions was incorporating gamification, such as earning badges for completing modules and progress bars to visualize learning milestones. This was inspired by user feedback indicating a preference for interactive and engaging learning experiences.

üë©üèª‚Äçüíª Following our brainstorming session, we began the design process by wireframing our ideas in Figma. This approach allowed us to swiftly validate and iterate on the user experience while maintaining a consistent and polished design throughout.

Challenges We Ran Into

  • All five of us joined the hackathon virtually, so there was a learning curve with figuring out our workflow at the beginning.
  • For most of the team it was their first time participating in a hackathon, and although that is a challenge, it is one we gladly took on to create something we are passionate about!
  • We had varying levels of experience with Figma, which presented a great opportunity for team learning. The more experienced members mentored those new to the platform, and we all benefited from the fresh perspectives.

What We Learned

Throughout our development of CashSmart, we learned how to create an app from the ground up using Figma. Our user interviews highlighted a significant demand for a platform that supports young people‚Äôs financial literacy in a personalized way. We found that many mainstream solutions were not meeting this need effectively due to a lack of inclusivity. In response, we developed CashSmart to offer a customizable experience where users can choose learning paths specifically tailored to their individual needs. Our application features straightforward videos that demystify complex financial concepts, accompanied by quizzes that help reinforce and apply what users learn. These elements are designed not only to educate but also to empower young people, enhancing their financial literacy and promoting inclusivity within the financial education space.

What's Next for CashSmart

  • Collaborating with developers to bring CashSmart to life, ensuring the implementation of key features and functionality.
  • Focusing on business goals to drive user conversion and retention, utilizing strategies to attract and engage a wider user base.
  • Expanding CashSmart's features to establish genuine financial confidence and independence, such as incorporating goal tracking, personalized budgeting techniques, and interactive reminders.
  • Continuously refining and enhancing CashSmart based on user feedback, aiming to provide the best possible experience and value for our users' financial journeys.
